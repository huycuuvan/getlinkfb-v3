Privacy Policy for OHARI PROD

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OHARI PROD ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you interact with our Facebook Messenger application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect public profile information that you provide via Facebook Messenger, which may include:

  • Your Name
  • Your Facebook User ID
  • Your profile picture (public)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To identify customer conversations in our CRM system.
  • To provide better and faster customer support.
  • To associate your messages with your customer profile.

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is stored securely on our private servers and is only accessible by authorized customer service personnel. We retain this data as long as necessary to provide you with support.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through the Facebook Page.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
